Crystal Collector Game

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Player will click on their chosen crystal that will add a generated random hidden number that will add the the players amount.

Player wins if they match their amount to the computers amounts. Player loses if they go over the target number.

The crystal value is hidden, when you click on it you can learn the crystals value.

Once the game stats over, the value of the crystals will change again.
